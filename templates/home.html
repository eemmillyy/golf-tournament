About Lynn Haven Rotary Club

The Lynn Haven Rotary Club is a community service organization that brings together business and professional leaders to provide humanitarian services, encourage high ethical standards in all vocations, and help build goodwill and peace in the world. By participating in the Rotary Tournament, you are contributing to our efforts in serving the community.
